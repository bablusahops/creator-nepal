About Creator Nepal

Connecting Nepali Creators with Businesses Across Nepal.

Who We Are

Creator Nepal is a dedicated digital platform created to connect talented Nepali creators with businesses, startups, organizations and brands looking for creative collaboration. Our mission is to build a trusted community where creators can showcase their talent, build professional profiles and discover exciting opportunities while businesses can easily find the right creators for marketing, promotions and brand growth. Nepal is home to thousands of talented YouTubers, TikTok creators, Instagram influencers, photographers, videographers, graphic designers, musicians, gamers, educators and digital professionals. However, many creators struggle to find genuine collaboration opportunities, while businesses often spend significant time searching for suitable creators. Creator Nepal bridges this gap by bringing both communities together on one professional platform. We believe creativity has the power to transform businesses and inspire people. Our platform is designed to simplify creator-business collaboration through an easy-to-use interface, transparent communication and trusted profiles. Whether you are a beginner creator or an established influencer, Creator Nepal provides a place to grow your career and connect with meaningful opportunities. Our long-term vision is to become Nepal's leading creator marketplace where businesses and creators work together to build successful partnerships, support local talent and strengthen Nepal's digital economy.
